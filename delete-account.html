Data Deletion Instructions

App Name: HangApp

Developer: ellenmartin11

At HangApp, we believe you should have full control over your data. This page provides clear instructions on how to request the deletion of your account and associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)
  1. Open the HangApp on your mobile device.
  2. Tap the Menu (FAB) button.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm your choice to permanently delete your data.
Option 2: Delete via Email Request

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us via our developer profile on the Google Play Store. Please include your registered email address.

Data Deleted and Retention

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed from our active servers:

  • User Profile: Your username, email address, and authentication credentials.
  • Check-in History: All past location check-ins and messages.
  • Friendships: All connections and pending friend requests.
  • Attendee Data: Any "I'm coming" records associated with your account.

Retention Period: Once you initiate deletion, your data is removed from our live databases almost immediately. For technical and security reasons (e.g., database backups), it may take up to 30 days for all traces to be completely purged from our systems.

Important Note

Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once your data is deleted, it cannot be recovered.
